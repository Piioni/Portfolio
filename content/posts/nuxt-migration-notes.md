The migration was not just a framework swap. It was an opportunity to redesign the frontend with better boundaries.

## What improved

- Content moved to markdown with typed collections
- Repeated UI patterns extracted into base components
- Home page structured as independent sections

## Why this matters

A portfolio tends to grow over time. If structure is weak, every new section adds chaos.
